Sieve of Eratosthenes

The Sieve of Eratosthenes is an ancient algoritm for finding all prime numbers up to any given limit. It does so by iteratively marking as composite (i. e. not primes) the multiples of each prime, starting with the first number, 2. The multiples of a given prime are generated as a sequence of numbers starting from that prime, with constant difference between them that is equal to prime.
